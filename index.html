Twitch HQ


60 conference tables


Twitch focused on design,
authenticity and function. Hashi was able to meet that need. It’s unique cable management system assured that the function and User Experience were planned for and executed.

Designed by Iwamoto Scott

Slack HQ


80 conference tables


Slack’s new HQ in San Franciso was designed to keep consistent functionality but varied aesthetic. The same Hashi conference tables were installed in different finishes on each floor.

Designed by O+A

Zoox HQ


500 workstations +
30 conference tables

To accommodate it’s growing team Zoox’s HQ in Foster City, installed 500 workstations with the Balance Beam along with 30 Hashi tables for meetings.

Designed by Rapt Studio
